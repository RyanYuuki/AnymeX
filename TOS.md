## Third-Party Services and Data Sources
AnymeX integrates with a number of third-party services and APIs to enhance its functionality. These integrations are used solely for informational, tracking, and enrichment purposes. AnymeX does not claim ownership of any data provided by these services. By using AnymeX, you agree to comply with the terms of service of each applicable third-party service listed below.

### Tracking Platforms
AnymeX connects to the following services for core tracking functionality:
- [**AniList**](https://anilist.co): Anime, manga, and novel tracking
- [**MyAnimeList**](https://myanimelist.net): Anime, manga and novel tracking
- [**Simkl**](https://simkl.com): Movie, series, and anime tracking

### Anime & Manga Metadata
AnymeX uses the following services to retrieve supplementary metadata and enrichment data:
- [**LiveChart**](https://livechart.me): Used to retrieve dubbed anime information and anime cover images
- [**AnimeSchedule**](https://animeschedule.net): Used to retrieve dubbed anime scheduling information
- [**MangaBaka**](https://mangabaka.dev/): Used to retrieve anime adaptation details for manga and light novel entries, as well as manga and novel news
- [**MangaUpdates**](https://mangaupdates.com): Used in conjunction with MangaBaka API for next chapter release date prediction data
- [**Chiaki**](https://chiaki.site/): Used to retrieve anime watch order information
- [**AnimeThemes**](https://animethemes.moe): Used to retrieve anime opening and ending theme information

### AI Recommendations
- [**AniBrain**](https://anibrain.ai): Used to power AI-based anime picks and personalized recommendations

### News
- [**Kuroiru**](https://kuroiru.co/app): Used to retrieve the latest anime news
- [**MangaBaka**](https://mangabaka.dev/): Also used to retrieve the latest manga and novel news

### List Export
- [**MALScraper**](https://malscraper.azurewebsites.net): Used to enable exporting of AniList and MyAnimeList anime and manga lists

### Book & Novel Discovery
- [**Google Books**](https://books.google.com): Used in the barcode scanner feature to look up books and novels
- [**OpenLibrary**](https://openlibrary.org): Used in the barcode scanner feature as a supplementary book data source

## Comments System & Comment Policy

AnymeX includes a built-in comments system powered by a third-party backend service (Commentum v2) to allow users to discuss anime, manga, novels, movies, and series.

### How Comment Data Is Handled

When you use the comments feature, the following information is processed and stored by the comment backend:
- Your platform user ID (from AniList, MAL, or Simkl)
- Your username
- Your profile avatar URL
- The comments you post
- Votes (upvotes/downvotes) on comments
- Associated media information (ID, title, type, poster, year)

AnymeX does NOT store:
- Your authentication tokens
- Your passwords
- Browsing history
- Watch history
- Personal data beyond what is listed above
